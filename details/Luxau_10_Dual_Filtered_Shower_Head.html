Luxau 10" Dual Filtered Shower Head

Experience the ultimate in shower hygiene with our advanced filtration and innovative design.

Advanced Filtration for Safer Showering

This premium shower head features a 20-stage water filter that effectively removes chlorine, heavy metals, and impurities, ensuring cleaner and healthier water for your family. The dual filtering system also helps in softening the water, reducing lime scale buildup, and making your shower experience more comfortable. Say goodbye to harsh chemicals that can dry your skin and damage your hair, and hello to a refreshing, gentle flow that nurtures your wellbeing.

Multi-Functionality for Every Need

With 8 powerful shower modes and 2 versatile household cleaning modes, the Luxau shower head is designed to handle a variety of tasks. From a soothing rain shower to a focused massage setting, you can customize your experience for optimal relaxation. The household cleaning modes make it easy to tackle tricky areas like corners, bathtubs, and even pet hair, all without taking your hand off the shower head.

Durable & Flexible Design

Equipped with an extra-long 79" explosion-proof steel-wire hose, this shower head offers exceptional flexibility and durability. Whether you need to adjust your position or take the shower head with you to clean different surfaces, the robust construction ensures long-lasting performance. The diverter control allows quick and seamless switches between handheld and fixed shower modes, or even using both simultaneously, though water pressure may be reduced when both outlets are in use.

Elegant & Functional

Designed for both style and utility, the 10" wide, chrome-plated shower head adds a touch of sophistication to any bathroom. Its broad surface ensures a steady and even water distribution, making it ideal for those who value both aesthetics and performance. Whether you prefer a luxurious rain shower or a targeted cleaning spray, the Luxau shower head delivers it all with a sleek and modern look.

Why Choose Luxau?

The Luxau 10" dual filtered shower head is the perfect blend of innovation, durability, and style. Ideal for both hard and soft water, it ensures a consistently soft and clean water flow, protecting your skin and hair from harmful impurities. With multiple shower modes and a robust hose, it’s designed to make your daily routine more convenient and enjoyable.
